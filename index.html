About Us


Dominion Family Services was founded by Fr. Michael Orr in 1994. Our initial goal was to provide support and education to domestic violence perpetrators. Since domestic violence and substance misuse are frequently intertwined, we began a substance abuse education program over ten years ago.

Dominion Family Services has been offering Batterer Intervention Programs (BIP) for men and women who have either been arrested on domestic violence charges or who voluntarily want to make changes in their lives.

Mission


Our mission at Dominion Family Services is to aid in building healthier lives, through person centered programming, to free yourself from domestic violence, anger and/or substance misuse.

Services


Substance Abuse

Dominion Family Services goal is to provide effective services for alcohol and other drug dependencies. We provide a variety of support and education services such as cognitive behavioral therapy, support groups, alcohol + other drug prevention, outpatient treatment, individual sessions, assessments and screening. Each person’s program is person centered because no two people are the same neither is their addiction therefore everybody’s program should be individualized. The overall focus for everybody is to empower individuals to rebuild their lives and learn how to live a productive substance free lifestyle.


Family Services


Dominion Family Services offers support for the families and we encourage family participation. Call us to schedule an appointment even if your substance user is not ready for recovery. Dominion Family Services can offer you the support and education you need to help your loved one.

Services


Domestic Violence


Domestic Violence is the use of intentional emotional, psychological, sexual or physical force by one family member or intimate partner to control another. Domestic Violence happens to everybody regardless of age, gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, education or socioeconomic status.

Dominion Family Services offers support groups for both men and women with anger issues or domestic violence concerns. We also offer individual sessions to fit your schedule.

Batterers Intervention Programs


Our Batterers Intervention Programs (BIP) are 26 to 52 weeks long. Anger management usually runs 16 weeks. Program length depends upon decision of the appropriate court.

Aftercare services are available through an alumni organization and alumni are welcome to return to group at anytime without a cost.

Join a support group. It is a good way to express your concern with people who have common experience and offer each other encouragement, comfort and advice.
